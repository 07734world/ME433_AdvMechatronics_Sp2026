MACBETH AND CHEESE

A warm and hearty Scottish stew for the power aspiring, best prepared between three enchanting friends. Makes 1 brew.

INGREDIENTS
1 cup Poisn'd toad
1/2 lb Fillet of fenny snake
4 Eye of newt
3 oz Toe of frog
2 tsbp Wool of bat
2 Tongue of dog
1 Adder's fork
3/4 cup Blind-worm's sting
2 Lizard's leg
5 oz Owlet's wing
5 Scale of dragon
1/2 cup Tooth of wolf
3 Witches' mummy
1 Maw and gulf of ravin'd salt-sea shark
4 oz Root of hemlock
1 tsp Gall of goat
2 Slips of yew
1/4 cup Tiger's chaudron
3 cup Gouda

INSTRUCTIONS
Bring cauldron to boil
Throw in poisn'd toad
Allow cauldron to bubble
Boil and bake in fillet of fenny snake
Mix in eye of newt, toe of frog, wool of bat, tongue of dog, adder's fork, blind-worm's sting, lizard's leg, owlet's wing
Allow to boil and bubble
Add scale of dragon, tooth of wolf, witches' mummy, maw and gulf of ravin'd salt-sea shark
Stir until gruel is thick and slab
Add tiger's chaudron
Toil and trouble, toil and trouble
Cool with gouda until firm and good

Serve with dash of premonition, to taste.
